Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Ingredients:
    • Spaghetti
    • Ground beef
    • Tomato sauce
    • Onions
    • Garlic
  • Instructions:
    1. Boil water and cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
    2. In a pan, brown the ground beef with chopped onions and garlic.
    3. Add tomato sauce and let it simmer.
    4. Combine the cooked spaghetti with the sauce.
