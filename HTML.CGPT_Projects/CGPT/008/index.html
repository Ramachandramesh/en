Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a large pan, brown the ground beef. Add chopped onion and garlic, cook until softened.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Serve the Bolognese sauce over cooked spaghetti.
